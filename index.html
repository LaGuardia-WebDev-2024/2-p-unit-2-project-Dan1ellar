The Food

Paris has a wide selection of cuisines to choose from! They have everything from traditional French food to authentic Vietnamese pho. They also have amazing pastries at boulangaries and beautfiul cafes to sit at for hours.

Scenery

Paris is a very old city, which makes it a very charming place. Each arrondissement (district) has its own unique speclialties as well.

Museums

Paris is home to one of the most famous museums in the world, the Louvre. It is also home to many other museums such as the Musee d'Orsay and the Grand Palais.
